## Content Insights
I find it useful that there are just three main questions to ask when doing a stand-up meeting. It ensures that the team stays on track, which is something I've found is difficult to do when working with teams, especially when getting a start on a task or knowing what tasks need to be completed.

Whilst doing my first official meeting with my group, I found that doing the 'Group Agreement' section on the Trello board was very useful for ensuring we have ways for resolving conflicts or getting someone to do work if they are not up-to-date.

## Career/Employability/Learning Insights
I have found that it is important to do stand-up meetings since it is a general practise in IT development workplaces. It will be important to show my confidence in doing these meetings for employment.
